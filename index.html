This is Gravity Chess, it gets its name from gravity.
All the pieces (except for pawns and kings) are affected by gravity, meaning they will fall down.
The white pieces will fall to the white side and the black pieces to the black side.
This has some interesting implications, namely:
  • Bishops can change what color they're on.
  • Rooks are kind of like lasers, and can't get off the back rank.
  • Pawns are way more important and are possibly even necessary for a checkmate.
  • Knights are basically useless, since they need to jump up on other pieces.
I have no clue if this is a functional version of chess or not, but I wanted to make this variant, so I did.
